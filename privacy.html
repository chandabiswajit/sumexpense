Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

SumExpense ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SumExpense mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile picture associated with your Google Account used for authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within SumExpense. AdMob uses cookies or unique device identifiers to show ads based on your interests and to measure the performance of those ads.

  • AdMob may collect data such as IP address, advertising ID, and app interaction data.
  • You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's "Google Settings" -> "Ads" and enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
